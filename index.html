How It Works

Three steps. Zero config.

1
Plug in camera
Connect your Kodak Charmera via USB. The menu bar icon turns gold.
2
Click the K
One click imports everything. Orientation fixed, videos converted, Photos.app updated.
3
Photos go live
Your retro contact sheet gallery publishes to GitHub Pages automatically. Share the link.
Features

Everything the camera
should have shipped with.

K
Menu Bar Native
Lives in your menu bar. Gray when idle, gold when camera is connected, blue during import.
↻
Auto Orientation
Apple Vision detects faces, text, and horizon lines to fix rotation automatically.
▸
Video Conversion
Converts the Charmera's AVI files to web-ready MP4. Bundled ffmpeg, zero setup.
□
Photos.app Import
Every import goes to a "Charmera" album in Photos.app. Your library stays organized.
⦿
GitHub Pages Gallery
Your photos publish to username.github.io/charmera-gallery. Retro Kodak contact sheet design. You own it.
#
Duplicate Detection
SHA-256 hashing prevents re-imports. Click as many times as you want.
▿
Camera Cleanup
Deletes photos from the camera after successful import. Ready for the next roll.
♥
Free & Open Source
MIT licensed. No accounts besides GitHub. No tracking. No cloud lock-in.
